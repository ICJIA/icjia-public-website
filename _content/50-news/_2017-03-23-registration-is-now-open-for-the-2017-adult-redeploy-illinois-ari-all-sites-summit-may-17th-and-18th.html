The sixth annual All-Sites Summit will take place on May 17th and 18th in Bloomington, Illinois and will feature training relevant to the breadth of Adult Redeploy Illinois (ARI) programs. This year’s Summit will focus on peer learning around innovations at ARI sites, community reintegration, and partnerships. An anticipated 150-170 people from all corners of Illinois will participate including ARI sites, partners and policy makers.

The sixth annual All-Sites Summit will take place on May 17th and 18th in Bloomington, Illinois and will feature training relevant to the breadth of Adult Redeploy Illinois (ARI) programs. This year’s Summit will focus on peer learning around innovations at ARI sites, community reintegration, and partnerships. An anticipated 150-170 people from all corners of Illinois will participate including ARI sites, partners and policy makers.

Visit the 2017 All-Sites Summit websites for full information and to register >>
