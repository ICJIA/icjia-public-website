The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority Office of Public Information is seeking an independent contractor who is an experienced copy editor to assist in editing research articles, reports, and website content. The ideal candidate is a confident copy editor with a sharp eye for detail and the ability to work well under pressure. This individual must be able to read and comprehend large (50+ page), detailed research reports and offer feedback and constructive criticism to researchers. This copy editor must be comfortable working closely with authors, editing for conciseness and readability by set deadlines.
