The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority (the Authority), based in downtown Chicago, is seeking a qualified candidate for the position of Criminal Justice Specialist in the Research and Analysis Unit. Under the general supervision of the Center for Criminal Justice Data and Analytics manager, the Criminal Justice Specialist performs complex statistical analysis and data management on large files of criminal justice data in support of the Unit's research and policy analysis agenda, and the Authority's funding decisions. This includes performing various regression modelling techniques, including logistic regression, hierarchical linear modeling, time series, survival analysis and other related techniques, using SPSS and R, to answer complex research questions, produce analyses of crime trends, provide feedback on the performance of Authority grant-funded projects, and provide general statistical expertise to the Research and Analysis Unit. The Criminal Justice Specialist also reviews professional literature on criminology, social science, statistical methods and economic theory in order to design appropriate research methodologies and writes reports and articles to be published to the Authority's website, and makes presentations of results and findings to various audiences upon request.
