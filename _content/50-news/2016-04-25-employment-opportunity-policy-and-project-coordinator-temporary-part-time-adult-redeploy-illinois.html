The Adult Redeploy Illinois (ARI) program housed at the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority is seeking an independent contractor for temporary and part-time policy and project coordinator duties (approximately June - August 2016). The policy and project coordinator must have experience in and knowledge of criminal justice reform generally and in particular alternatives to incarceration. ARI is a program within the Authority consisting of state funding and support to 20 county-level community corrections programs in Illinois.

The Adult Redeploy Illinois (ARI) program housed at the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority is seeking an independent contractor for temporary and part-time policy and project coordinator duties (approximately June - August 2016). The policy and project coordinator must have experience in and knowledge of criminal justice reform generally and in particular alternatives to incarceration. ARI is a program within the Authority consisting of state funding and support to 20 county-level community corrections programs in Illinois.
