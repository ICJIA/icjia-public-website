The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority Budget Committee will meet on Tuesday, January 19, 2016 at 9:00 a.m. at the offices of the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority 300 W. Adams, Suite 200, Large Conference Room, Chicago, Illinois, 60606.

The materials for the January 19, 2016 Budget Committee meeting have been revised. Changes include:

  • VADGT memo now reflects request for designations to be retroactive to 1/1/16.
  • VAWA memo now reflects request for designations to be retroactive to 1/1/16.
  • VOCA memo now reflects request for designations to be retroactive to 1/1/16 and now includes a previously omitted designation request for funding to Life Span as part of the Cook County Domestic Violence MDT group of designations.
