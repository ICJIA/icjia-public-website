The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority is seeking a qualified candidate for the position of grantee auditor in its Office of Fiscal Information. Under the general supervision of the accounting manager, the grantee auditor performs financial audits of federal and state grantees ensuring procedures, policies, legislation and regulations are followed; prepares detailed reports on audit findings; maintains, examines, and reconciles general account books, records, and documents, and examines, reconciles, and posts information from detailed account books and related reports.
