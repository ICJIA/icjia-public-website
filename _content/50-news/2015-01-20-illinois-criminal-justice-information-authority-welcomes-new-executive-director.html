CHICAGO--January 20, 2015. The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority (ICJIA) is pleased to announce John Maki has been appointed ICJIA Executive Director. Mr. Maki was nominated to the position by Governor Bruce Rauner on January 10, 2015.

John Maki, Executive Director, ICJIA
