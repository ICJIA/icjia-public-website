Lunch & Learn - Bring your lunch and join us for a presentation:

NARCOTICA: INVESTIGATIVE JOURNALISM ON PUBLIC HEALTH AND CRIMINAL JUSTICE

Friday, November 2, 2018
12 p.m. to 1:30 p.m.

ICJIA Headquarters
300 W. Adams St., 2nd Fl. Conference Room
Chicago, Illinois
