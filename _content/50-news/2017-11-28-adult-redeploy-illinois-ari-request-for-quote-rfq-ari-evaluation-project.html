The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority (ICJIA) is a state agency responsible for supporting programs in the criminal justice system that utilize sanctions in the most efficient and cost-effective manner possible, while maintaining public safety. ICJIA seeks to support a process evaluation of four Adult Redeploy Illinois intensive supervision probation with services programs. These programs reside in the following Illinois counties: DuPage, Macon, Peoria, and St. Clair. Quotes must be submitted no later than December 13, 2017 at 1:00 p.m.
