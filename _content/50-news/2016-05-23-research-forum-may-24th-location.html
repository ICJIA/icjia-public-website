The ICJIA-sponsored Research Forum, Trauma and Posttraumatic Stress in Criminal Justice Populations, will be held on Tuesday, May 24, 2016. Registration will begin at 8:30am and the forum begin at 9 a.m and end at 12 p.m. The location was changed to Loyola University Chicago, Water Tower Campus, 25 E. Pearson St., Philip H. Corboy Law Center, Power Rogers & Smith Ceremonial Courtroom, 10th floor Chicago, IL.

To register to attend in person or the webinar, or for more information, go to http://www.icjia.state.il.us/sites/trauma-and-ptsd
