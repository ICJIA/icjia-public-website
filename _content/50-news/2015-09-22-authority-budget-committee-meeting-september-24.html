The Authority’s Budget Committee will meet on Thursday, September 24, 2015 at 1:00 p.m. at the Authority’s Offices, 300 W. Adams, Second Floor, Chicago, Illinois, 60606.

Agenda items will include:

  • Public Comment
  • Minutes of the June 19, 2015 Budget Committee Meeting
  • Minutes of the August 6, 2015 Budget Committee Meeting
  • Justice Assistance Grants (JAG)
  • National Forensic Sciences Improvement Act (NFSIA)
  • Residential Substance Abuse Treatment (RSAT) Act
  • Violence Against Women Act (VAWA)
  • Violence Against Women Act (VAWA) Sexual Assault Services Program (SASP)

View the meeting agenda and materials.
