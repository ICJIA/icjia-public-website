The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority received a solicited award notification from the U.S. Department of Justice Office of Justice Programs requesting an application for the use of FFY16 Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act (SORNA) funds to be passed through to Illinois State Police (ISP). The Authority and ISP developed and submitted an application for SORNA funding and $249,576 were awarded to ICJIA to administer program. With a completed application, these funds will be granted to ISP for staff overtime to scan backlogged paper records into its Sex Offender Registration and Notification Unit electronic filing system.
