--- title: Victim Policy Advisor _template: employment siteLink: http://agency.governmentjobs.com/illinois/job_bulletin.cfm?JobID=1939151 super_positive_keywords: - Employment Opportunity --- The Victim Policy Advisor position is a key level position in the ongoing understanding and implementation of victims' service policy projects at the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority. This position will analyze complex policy, best practices and programmatic information; work on an interdisciplinary team to ensure that outside stakeholders and grantees are effectively engaged; identify key policy issues as well as coordinating and conducting trainings and meetings; assist in meeting key needs for ICJIA's effective implementation of victim service funding by identifying policy and programmatic issues; meet with stakeholders and other experts to best address emerging issues.
