The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority’s Family Violence Coordinating Council program is seeking an independent contractor who is experienced in family violence or related field in the areas of program development and delivery. The ideal candidate will have experience in grants management and federal grants as well as competencies and skills associated with providing leadership. The position is half-time (average of 20 hours per week), reporting to ICJIA’s Springfield office, schedule is negotiable.
