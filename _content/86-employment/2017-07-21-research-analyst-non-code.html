--- title: 'Research Analyst - Non-Code ' _template: employment siteLink: https://agency.governmentjobs.com/illinois/job_bulletin.cfm?JobID=1800557 super_positive_keywords: - Employment Opportunity - Research Analyst --- The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority ("the Authority"), based in downtown Chicago, is seeking a qualified candidate for the position of Research Analyst. Under the direction of the manager of the Center for Justice Research and Evaluation, performs a variety of senior level technical functions relating to the design, development, implementation, and monitoring of applied research and process and impact evaluations of criminal justice-related programs and practices. The person in the position will prepare, present and disseminate research findings; perform reviews of relevant criminal justice literature; develop and use appropriate and sound methodology to design and manage a research project, perform simple to moderately complex statistical analyses of criminal justice and non-criminal justice data sets utilizing SPSS and other software in conjunction with process and impact evaluation work and to support the agency's data archive; assist in the development of Notification of Funding Opportunities (NOFO), Request for Applications (RFA), or Request for Proposals (RFP) of agency-funded programs. Provide reviews of relevant literature to guide NOFO, RFA, and RFT development; serve as a liaison to criminal justice agencies, organizations, and associations; perform other duties as required or assigned which are reasonably within the scope of the duties enumerated above.
