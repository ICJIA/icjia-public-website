The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority (“the Authority”), based in downtown Chicago, is seeking a qualified candidate for the position of Criminal Justice Specialist in the Research and Evaluation Center. Under the general supervision of the Illinois Criminal Justice Research and Evaluation Center manager, the Criminal Justice Specialist conducts research and evaluation through the development of research methodologies, creation of surveys, and collection of data. Produces written documents and reports that share research design, provides background information, and summarizes research findings.
