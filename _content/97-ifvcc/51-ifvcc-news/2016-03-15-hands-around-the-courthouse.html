The Youth Services Network and Peoria County Government will host Hands Around the Courthouse at noon on April 15, 2016.

This event is hosted by Youth Services Network and Peoria County as a collaborative effort to bring awareness to the community regarding child abuse. Spotlight is on the number of child abuse and neglect cases currently pending in Peoria County and how the community as a whole can come together to address this issue for all Peoria County residents. The event will be held at the Peoria County Courthouse, 324 N. Main St. Peoria, Illinois.

The Youth Services Network and Peoria County Government will host 'Hands Around the Courthouse' at noon on April 15, 2016.

This event is hosted by Youth Services Network and Peoria County as a collaborative effort to bring awareness to the community regarding child abuse. Spotlight is on the number of child abuse and neglect cases currently pending in Peoria County and how the community as a whole can come together to address this issue for all Peoria County residents. The event will be held at the Peoria County Courthouse, 324 N. Main St. Peoria, Illinois.
