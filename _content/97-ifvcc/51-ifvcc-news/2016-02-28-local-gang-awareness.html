Presentation by Kankakee City Police department Gang Specialist on Community Strategies to reduce the adverse impact of gangs, their associated violence, and their devastating life-long impacts. The focus will on the 2 primary gangs in the comminity.

The presentation will be held on March 1, 2016 from 12 noon to 1:30pm at the Donald E. Green Public Safety Building, 385 East Oak.
