Resources

The National Consortium for the Study of Terrorism and Responses to Terrorism (START) is a University of Maryland-based research and education center comprised of an international network of scholars committed to the scientific study of the causes and human consequences of terrorism in the United States and around the world. A Department of Homeland Security Center of Excellence, START supports the research efforts of leading social scientists at more than 50 academic and research institutions and serves as a leading resource for homeland security policymakers and practitioners.
