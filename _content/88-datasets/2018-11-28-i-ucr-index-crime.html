This dataset is prepared and published by Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority (ICJIA). Visit http://icjia.state.il.us to learn more about ICJIA.

Index offenses include Violent Index offenses (murder, criminal sexual assault, robbery, aggravated assault) and Property Index offenses (burglary, theft, motor vehicle theft, arson). ICJIA uses the latest data published in Crime in Illinois by the Illinois State Police and the latest population estimates provided by the National Center for Health Statistics.

Index arrests include Violent Index offenses (murder, criminal sexual assault, robbery, aggravated assault) and Property Index arrests (burglary, theft, motor vehicle theft, arson). ICJIA uses the latest data published in Crime in Illinois by the Illinois State Police and the latest population estimates provided by the National Center for Health Statistics.
