The Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority is requesting proposals for SORNA Electronic Transmission and Livescan grants. Grants will be made with SORNA Federal Fiscal Year 2016 and 2017 funds. A total of $463,307 in funding is available through this solicitation for use over a period of six months. Application deadline: December 21, 2017
