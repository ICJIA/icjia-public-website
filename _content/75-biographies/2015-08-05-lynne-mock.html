Lynne Mock is an authority research analyst. Her areas of interest include mental health, violence prevention, juvenile justice, applied research, program evaluation. Ms. Mock received a Bachelors of Arts in Psychology from the University of Illinois Chicago and a Doctorate of Philosophy in Psychology from the University of Illinois Chicago.
