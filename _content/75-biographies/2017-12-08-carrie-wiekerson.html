Carrie Wiekerson is a criminal justice specialist with ICJIA’s Adult Redeploy Illinois program. After a 20-year career in the Sears advertising department, Carrie returned to college at Northeastern Illinois University, where she earned her bachelor’s degree in Sociology with a minor in human resource development and a master’s degree in criminal justice and training. Prior to joining ICJIA in 2017, Carrie worked as an administrator for the provost at City Colleges of Chicago. She has a passion for seeing people’s lives made whole and making positive change through knowledge and training, which infuses her work as an ARI grants monitor.
