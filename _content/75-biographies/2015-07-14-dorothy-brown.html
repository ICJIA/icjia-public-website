--- title: Dorothy Brown _template: biography sortField: Brown unit: ' ' role: Board modifier: ' ' organization: ICJIA authorPhoto: /assets/img/biographies/brown-20150714094936.jpg --- Dorothy Brown was first elected Cook County Circuit Clerk in 2000, becoming the first African American to hold the position. She was re-elected in 2004 and again in 2008. Ms. Brown holds a master's in business administration, a juris doctorate, and is a certified public accountant. As the official keeper of records for all judicial matters brought into one of the largest unified court systems in the world, Ms. Brown manages an annual operating budget of more than $100 million and a workforce of more than 2,300 employees.
