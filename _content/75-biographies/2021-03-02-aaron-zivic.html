Prior to joining the agency, Aaron worked as an attorney within the Illinois Department of Financial and Professional Regulation Banking Division. Before that, Aaron served as an administrative law judge at the Illinois Department of Healthcare and Family Services presiding over Medicaid matters. He earned a juris doctor at Chicago-Kent College of Law, a master’s degree in public administration at University of Illinois-Chicago, and a bachelor’s degree in political science at Washington University.
