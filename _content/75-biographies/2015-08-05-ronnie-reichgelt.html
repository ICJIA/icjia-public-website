Ronnie J. Reichgelt is a program manager for the Victims of Crime Act (VOCA) program in the Federal & State Grants Unit, a position he has held since 2006. Ronnie joined ICJIA in 1999 as a victim service specialist monitoring Violence Against Women Act and VOCA grants and was promoted to Justice Assistance Grant program administrator in 2005. Ronnie has a bachelor’s degree in history from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a master’s degree in history and in public administration from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee.
