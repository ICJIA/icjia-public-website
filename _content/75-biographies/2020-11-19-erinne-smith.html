Erinne Smith is a Research Analyst in ICJIA’s Center for Victim Studies. Her research interests include human trafficking, reproductive rights, social justice, data analysis, and sociological theory. She has a bachelor’s degree in sociology from Middle Tennessee State University. Erinne has a strong background in qualitative and quantitative analysis.
