Before joining ICJIA, Christian was the director of external affairs and communications for a Cook County commissioner. In that role, he served as lead legislative liaison and coordinated all office communications and constituent services. His work as an organizer and communications professional centers around increasing community input to improve policy making. He has a bachelor’s degree in political science from Trinity Christian College and is a master of public administration candidate at Norther Illinois University.
