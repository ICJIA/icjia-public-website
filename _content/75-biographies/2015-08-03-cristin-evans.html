Cristin Evans first joined ICJIA as a technical editor in 1998. As the agency’s public information officer, Cristin coordinates communication between ICJIA and criminal justice practitioners, policymakers, and members of the public. Prior to joining the agency, Cristin was a journalist with the Downers Grove Reporter and the Daily Herald. Cristin has a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Columbia College Chicago.
