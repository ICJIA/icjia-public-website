Cristin Evans first joined the Authority as a technical editor in 1998. As the agency’s public information officer, Cristin coordinates communication between the Authority and criminal justice practitioners, policymakers, and members of the public. Prior to joining the Authority, Cristin was a journalist with the Downers Grove Reporter and the Daily Herald. She is a member of the Riverside School District 96 Wellness Committee and Riverside Junior Women’s Charity. She has a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Columbia College Chicago.
