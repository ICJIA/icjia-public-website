Governor Bruce Rauner reappointed Maureen Josh to the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority in August 2015. Ms. Josh has been the DeKalb County Circuit Clerk for more than 30 years. She is the keeper of court records and manages a team of 35 other clerks, while working with members and agencies of the court system, as well as the general public. Prior to becoming clerk, she worked as a secretary for the DeKalb County State’s Attorney. Ms. Josh previously served on the Authority from 2000 to 2006. She has a bachelor’s degree in education from the University of Wisconsin, Platteville.
