ICJIA is conducting a study on crime victims’ needs, organizational capacity, and gaps in services in Illinois.

ICJIA is looking for agencies who provide services to victims of crime in Illinois to participate in its survey of victim service providers.  The purpose of this study is to understand victim service providers’ perspectives on the needs of crime victims, providers’ capacity to meet these needs, and current gaps in services in Illinois.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may decide not to participate and this decision will not impact current or future grant funding from ICJIA, nor ICJIA’s relationship with you in any other way.

Should you decide to participate please click here

ICJIA is also interested in feedback from other providers serving crime victims.  Please feel free to share the electronic survey link with other victim service providers who serve victims of crime in Illinois.

For information or questions about the study, please contact the researchers at CJA.victimstudies@illinois.gov.
