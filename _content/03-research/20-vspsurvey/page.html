The Authority is conducting a study on violent crime victims’ needs, organizational capacity and gaps in services in Illinois. We are looking for agencies who provide services to victims of violent crime in Illinois to participate in our survey of victim service providers.

The purpose of the present study is to understand victim service providers’ perspective on the needs of violent crime victims, providers’ capacity to meet these needs, and gaps in services in Illinois.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may decide not to participate and this decision will not impact current or future grant funding from our agency, nor our relationship with you in any other way.

Should you decide to participate please click here

We are also interested in feedback of other providers serving crime victims in your area that we may not be aware of.  Please feel free to share the electronic survey link with other victim service providers who serve victims of violent crime in Illinois.

For information or questions about the study, please contact Dr. Jaclyn Houston-Kolnik at Jaclyn.kolnik@illinois.gov.
