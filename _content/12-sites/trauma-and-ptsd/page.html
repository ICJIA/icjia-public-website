Trauma and Posttraumatic Stress in Criminal Justice Populations

Presented by the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority
in collaboration with Adler University and Loyola University Chicago

This research forum will bring together researchers, practitioners, and policy makers to discuss trauma and posttraumatic stress of criminal justice populations. Attendees will learn about the latest research, policy implications, and innovative, evidence-based practices.

The event is free, space is limited.

If you require accommodations or use English as a second language, please email the organizer.

Tuesday, May 24, 2016 from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM (CDT) Chicago, IL
